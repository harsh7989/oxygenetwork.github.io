About us - Oxygen Network

About our server

Hey there! Welcome to the Oxygen Network, a public Minecraft server where players from around the world come together to build, explore, and create unforgettable memories. Whether you're new to Minecraft or a seasoned player, our server offers a welcoming community and a variety of gameplay options to suit your interests. We believe that Minecraft is more than just a game - it's an opportunity to connect with people from all walks of life and share in the joy of creating something truly unique.

Our Location in India

While our players come from all over the world, we're proud to call India our home. Our server is based in India, and we love sharing the beauty and diversity of our country with our community. From the bustling streets of Mumbai to the tranquil beaches of Goa, India is a land of contrasts and surprises. We hope that by hosting a Minecraft server here, we can help people from all over the world discover the magic of India for themselves.

Creating a Beautiful Minecraft Community

At the Oxygen Network, we're not just about playing Minecraft - we're about building a community. We believe that Minecraft is a tool for creativity and connection, and we're committed to fostering a positive and inclusive environment for all of our players. Whether you're building a castle, exploring a dungeon, or just hanging out with friends, our server is a place where you can be yourself and make lasting friendships..

Two Brothers Running the Server

The Oxygen Network was founded by two brothers with a passion for Minecraft and a dream of creating a thriving community. We've been running this server since 2021, and we're still going strong. We believe that our shared vision and complementary skills have helped us build a server that truly stands out from the crowd. Whether we're working on new plugins, organizing events, or just playing Minecraft together, we're always striving to make our server the best it can be.
